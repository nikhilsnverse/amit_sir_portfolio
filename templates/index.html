About Us

What is Teleradiology?

Teleradiology is the secure electronic transmission of radiological images — X-rays, CT scans, MRI, and Mammography — from one location to another for expert interpretation by specialized radiologists, eliminating geographical barriers to quality healthcare in India.

01

Image Capture

High-resolution medical images acquired using advanced digital modalities.

X-ray, CT, MRI, and Mammography images are captured by skilled technicians using state-of-the-art equipment following standardized protocols for optimal diagnostic quality.

02

Secure Transmission

Encrypted transfer via HIPAA-compliant PACS networks.

Images are securely transmitted with end-to-end encryption, ensuring patient confidentiality while enabling real-time access for radiologists anywhere in India.

03

Expert Interpretation

Board-certified radiologists analyze every study meticulously.

Our team of experienced radiologists brings subspecialty expertise to every case, ensuring accurate and comprehensive diagnostic interpretations.

04

Report Delivery

Detailed reports delivered with rapid turnaround times.

Comprehensive diagnostic reports are delivered securely via our platform, available 24/7 for timely clinical decision-making and improved patient outcomes.

What We Offer

Our Teleradiology Services

X-ray Reporting Services

Comprehensive interpretation of digital X-ray images with detailed reports and rapid turnaround time. Expert chest, musculoskeletal, and abdominal X-ray analysis.

CT Scan Analysis & Reporting

Expert analysis of CT scans including axial, coronal, and sagittal reconstructions for accurate diagnosis. Specialized in head, chest, abdomen, and musculoskeletal CT.

MRI Reporting Services

Detailed MRI reporting covering all sequences and protocols with subspecialist expertise. Brain, spine, joint, and body MRI interpretations.

Mammography Reporting

Expert mammography interpretation for breast cancer screening and diagnostic studies with detailed, accurate BI-RADS reporting.
