About Us

What is Teleradiology?

Teleradiology is the secure electronic transmission of radiological images X-rays, CT scans, MRI, and Mammography from one location to another for expert interpretation by specialized radiologists, eliminating geographical barriers to quality healthcare.

01

Image Capture

High-resolution medical images acquired using advanced digital modalities.

X-ray, CT, MRI, and Mammography images are captured by skilled technicians using state-of-the-art equipment following standardized protocols for optimal diagnostic quality.

02

Secure Transmission

Encrypted transfer via HIPAA-compliant PACS networks.

Images are securely transmitted with end-to-end encryption, ensuring patient confidentiality while enabling real-time access for radiologists anywhere in the world.

03

Expert Interpretation

Board-certified radiologists analyze every study meticulously.

Our team of experienced radiologists brings subspecialty expertise to every case, ensuring accurate and comprehensive diagnostic interpretations.

04

Report Delivery

Detailed reports delivered with rapid turnaround times.

Comprehensive diagnostic reports are delivered securely via our platform, available 24/7 for timely clinical decision-making and improved patient outcomes.
